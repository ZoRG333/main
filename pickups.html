This section details all the item pickups which you can find in the levels in Gore. The first set of items will be those which directly effect your character, and the last set will be ammo pickups.

Pickups in Gore can be destroyed to create an explosion which can both deny enemies a pickup as well as hurt them if the explosion was close enough to them.

Nano Heal

Instant boost of +20 Health.

Works up to a maximum of 100 Health.

Nano Stam

Instant boost of +20 Stamina.

Nano Boost

Instant boost of +5 Health and +10 Stamina.

Often found together in groups.

Nano Heal 100

Instant boost of 100 Health

Gives you 100 Health regardless of how much you have.

Nano Stam 100

Instant boost of 100 Stamina

Gives you 100 Stamina regardless of how much you have.

Invulnerability

Your player will be completely invulnerable to all damage.

While in effect you emit a loud sound as a warning to others.

Lasts for 30 seconds.

Cannot be destroyed.

MAD Damage

All attacks by your player will do much more damage than usual.

While in effect you glow and emit a loud sound as a warning to others.

Lasts for 25 seconds.

Cannot be destroyed.

Gas Mask

Replaces Helmet if you already had it.

Protects your head from physical harm.

Protects you from gas.

50 Armor Points.

Helmet

Protects your head from physical harm.

Does not protect against gas.

45 Armor Points.

Chest Armor

Protects your chest from physical harm.

100 Armor Points.

Leg Armor

Protects your legs from physical harm.

100 Armor Points. (50 per leg)

Bullet Ammo

+50 Ammo for Riotgun or Ingrams

+15 Ammo for Pistol

Shotgun Ammo

+12 Ammo for Shotguns

Rocket Ammo

+8 Rockets

+3 Hand Grenades

Minigun Ammo

+75 Minigun Ammo

Sniper \ Energy Ammo

+10 Energy Ammo
