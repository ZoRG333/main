What is a premium key?

(WARNING! The information bellow is outdated and has been kept only for archiving purposes. This is a fan restoration of the original Gore Special Edition page and we sell nothing. The premium key is included for FREE on our Gore Special Edition installer that can be found HERE). A premium key is a special serial number which you can enter into Gore to unlock new features. Entering it is a simple process that takes only seconds.

 

Why get a premium key?

As time goes on we will give Gore periodic updates. This will include better graphics, new features and possibly new game modes down the line as well.

However as new features come along, various great, select features in the future will be available exclusively to users who have a Premium Key. Here is a list of current premium key features:

 

  • Radar for seeing locations of your team mates, and flags in CTF.
  • See Health and Armor of living team mates by targeting them.
  • See Health and Armor of team mates while observing\dead.
  • Play vocal character taunts for all to hear at the push of a button, and call for a medic.

Note that orders are manually processed and can take up to 24 hours for delivery. Once your order is processed, you will get your premium key in your email.

 

Key Instructions:

Once you receive your key in email, launch Gore and wait for the main menu.

Next, click System Options, then Enter New Key. You will next see the following screen:

From here just click inside the red box, type in your key, and then hit enter. Once you have done this you are all finished, and the main menu should reappear.

Now just restart Gore and you will have premium features. The easiest immediate sign is seeing a Radar in your Dashboard while playing.
